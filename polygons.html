Definitions

Distance: Total distance (km).
Opportunity Cost: Cost of spending additional time commuting, which could have been used for work or leisure (INR).
Assumes equal probability of work and leisure and takes as inputs additional commuting hours and a user-supplied monthly wage using this page's slider.
Total Economic Cost: Opportunity cost plus additional fuel cost for the selected route (INR).
Total Environmental Cost: Total cost of additional CO2 emissions for the selected route (INR).
Note: Generalised exchange rate of USD 1 to INR 70.
